Garam Masala Dal Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Tuwar Dal
  • 1 and a half cups of water
  • 2 chunks of ginger garlic green chili paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 or more Tomatoes
  • 1/2 lime
  • 2 tablespoon of Garam Masala

Steps:

  1. Grab the cooker and put in Tuwar Dal and water
  2. Put it on high heat and let it whistle 40 times
  3. Put it off heat
  4. Open the cooker and mix it vigorously with a whisk
  5. Then put the contents into a pot
  6. Put the pot on high heat
  7. Put in the ginger garlic chili paste, salt, turmeric, lime and tomatoes
  8. Let it boil for 5 mins
  9. Turn off the gas
  10. Put in the Ghee and the Garam Masala

Serve and Enjoy!!!
