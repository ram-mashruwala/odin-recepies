Indian Style Roasted Potato Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium potatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon of "Methi"
  • 1/2 spoon of "Rai"
  • A dash of "Hing"
  • 3 chunks of ginger garlic green chili paste
  • As much turmeric as needed
  • As much "Dhaana Jeero" powder as needed
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of "Garam Masala"
  • 1 medium bunch of cilantros finely cut
  • 20 teaspoons of oil

Steps:

  1. Get out a non-stick pan and put it on high heat.
  2. Put the oil in the pan.
  3. Once the oil is heated, put in the Methi unil it has turned a deep black
  4. Put in the Hing then immediately put in the potatoes
  5. Cover it and let it cook until it is al dente
    • Also in short intervals, mix everything togeather
  6. Then put in the ginger garlick chili paste, salt, turmeric and Dhaana Jeero
  7. Put the lid back on and let it cook fully
  8. Then turn off the gas and then put in the Garam Masala and cilantro

Serve and Enjoy!!!
